# Apple Intelligence

## Source
[Wikipedia: Apple Intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_Intelligence) → raw excerpt at [wikipedia-apple-intelligence.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-apple-intelligence.md).

## Announcement (verbatim)
> "Announced on June 10, 2024, at the 2024 Worldwide Developers Conference"

## Architecture (verbatim)
> "Apple Intelligence comprises on-device and server-based foundation models."

## ChatGPT integration (verbatim)
> "System-wide integration with ChatGPT, allowing Siri to determine when to send certain complex user requests to ChatGPT. This system-wide integration is powered by GPT-4o."

## Future Gemini integration (verbatim)
> "Apple plans to integrate other models such as Google's Gemini into the system in the future."

> "In January 2026, Apple and Google announced a partnership where Apple's next-generation models are expected to incorporate Google's Gemini models."

## Features
Writing tools (proofread/rewrite), Image Playground (image generation), notification summarization, photo retouching, Genmoji, real-time translation, Mail categorization, Visual Intelligence (object recognition).

## Device compatibility (verbatim)
> "All iPhones, iPads, and Macs that have an M-series Apple Silicon chip or an A17 Pro or newer chip are supported."

iPhone 15 Pro and later; iPad Pro M1 and later; MacBook Air M1 and later.

## Significance
Apple Intelligence is the largest consumer-facing AI deployment by user count: it ships to all supported Apple devices automatically. The Apple-OpenAI partnership (GPT-4o integration) and the Apple-Google partnership (Gemini integration, January 2026) make Apple a meaningful frontier-AI distribution channel without operating a frontier-AI lab itself.
