# Google DeepMind

## Origin (per [Wikipedia: Google DeepMind](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google_DeepMind))
Raw excerpt: [wikipedia-google-deepmind.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-google-deepmind.md)

> "DeepMind Technologies was founded in London in 2010 by Demis Hassabis, Shane Legg, and Mustafa Suleyman."

DeepMind was acquired by Google in 2014.

## 2023 merger (verbatim)
> "In April 2023, DeepMind merged with Google AI's Google Brain division to form Google DeepMind, as part of the company's continued efforts to accelerate work on AI in response to OpenAI's ChatGPT."

> "DeepMind co-founder and head Demis Hassabis has been named as CEO of the new entity, while Brain's co-founder Jeff Dean will become chief scientist for both Google Research and Google DeepMind."

## Headquarters (verbatim)
> "Headquartered in London, with research centres in the United States, Canada, France, Germany, and Switzerland."

## Google Brain origin (verbatim)
> "Google Brain was initially established by Google Fellow Jeff Dean and visiting Stanford professor Andrew Ng."
