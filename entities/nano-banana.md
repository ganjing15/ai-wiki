# Nano Banana (Google DeepMind image generation)

## Source
[Wikipedia: Gemini (language model)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nano_Banana) — the URL `/wiki/Nano_Banana` redirects into the Gemini article; Nano Banana section saved at [wikipedia-nano-banana.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-nano-banana.md).
Additional: [blog.google Nano Banana 2](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/ai/nano-banana-2/)

## Developer
Google DeepMind.

## What it is
Nano Banana is a family of image generation and editing models embedded in the Gemini ecosystem. The nickname originated from "nicknames given to Naina Raisinghani, Product Manager at Google DeepMind."

## Model family

| Nickname | Official model name | Release date |
|----------|--------------------|----|
| Nano Banana | Gemini 2.5 Flash Image | 2025-08-26 |
| Nano Banana Pro | Gemini 3 Pro Image | 2025-11-20 |
| Nano Banana 2 | Gemini 3.1 Flash Image | 2026-02-26 |

## Release history
- **2025-08-12** — First appeared anonymously on the AI evaluation platform Arena
- **2025-08-26** — Public release through the Gemini app; became a viral sensation
- **2025-11-20** — Nano Banana Pro launched with improved text rendering and world knowledge
- **2026-02-26** — Nano Banana 2 released

## Impact (verbatim from Wikipedia)
> "Nano Banana became a viral Internet sensation on social media, particularly for its photorealistic '3D figurine' images."
> Helped "attract over 10 million new users to the Gemini app and facilitated more than 200 million image edits within weeks of launch."

## Capabilities
- Change hairstyles, backdrops, and mix photos with natural language cues
- Subject consistency across image revisions; multi-image fusion
- SynthID watermarking for AI-generated content identification
- **Nano Banana 2**: subject consistency for up to 5 characters and 14 objects; 512px–4K output; C2PA Content Credentials; real-time web search integration

## Nano Banana 2 verbatim quotes (Google blog)
> "Now you can get the advanced world knowledge, quality and reasoning you love in Nano Banana Pro, at lightning-fast speed."
> "Nano Banana 2 delivers vibrant lighting, richer textures and sharper details"

## Availability (Nano Banana 2)
Gemini app, Google Search, AI Studio, Gemini API, Google Cloud Vertex AI, Flow, Google Ads.
