# Cohere

## Source
[Wikipedia: Cohere](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cohere) → raw excerpt at [wikipedia-cohere.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-cohere.md).

## Founding (verbatim)
> "Cohere was founded in 2019 by Aidan Gomez, Ivan Zhang, and Nick Frosst" — headquartered in **Toronto**.

(Aidan Gomez was a co-author of the original "Attention Is All You Need" Transformer paper.)

## Offices
Toronto + Montreal, NYC, San Francisco, London, Paris, Seoul.

## Models
Command A, Command A Reasoning, Command A Vision, Cohere North, Aya Vision.

## Funding (verbatim)
- 2025: "$500 million at a $6.8 billion valuation"
- Follow-on: "$100 million" raising valuation to ~$7B
- February 2026 revenue: "$240 million"

## Positioning (verbatim)
> "An alternative to OpenAI"

Focus on enterprise / regulated industries: "finance, healthcare, manufacturing, and energy fields, as well as the public sector."

Partnerships: Oracle, Salesforce.

## Frontier AI Safety Commitments
Cohere is one of the **16 organizations** that signed the [Frontier AI Safety Commitments at the AI Seoul Summit (2024-05-21)](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/frontier-ai-safety-commitments-ai-seoul-summit-2024/frontier-ai-safety-commitments-ai-seoul-summit-2024).
