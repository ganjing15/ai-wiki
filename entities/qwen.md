## License (verbatim)
> "Apache 2.0 license, the source-available Qwen License, or the non-commercial Qwen Research License; other proprietary Qwen models are served through Alibaba Cloud."

Apache 2.0 variants: Qwen2 (7B, 1.5B, 0.5B), Qwen2.5-VL (7B, 32B), entire Qwen3 family.

## Specific models (verbatim)
- **QwQ-32B**: "performs better than o1 on some benchmarks"; "32K token context length"
- **Qwen3**: "trained on 36 trillion tokens in 119 languages and dialects"

## Significance
Alongside [[DeepSeek]], Qwen is one of the two major Chinese open-weight model families with broad international adoption. Qwen3's 36T-token training corpus exceeds reported sizes for most Western models.
