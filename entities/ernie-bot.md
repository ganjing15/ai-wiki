# Ernie Bot (Baidu)

## Source
[Wikipedia: Ernie Bot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernie_Bot) → raw excerpt at [wikipedia-ernie-bot.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-ernie-bot.md).

## Developer
Baidu.

## Launch (verbatim)
- Invited testing: **2023-03-16**
- Public release: **2023-08-31**, "after receiving approval from Chinese regulators"

## Model versions
| Version | Date |
|---------|------|
| Ernie 3.0 | initial launch |
| Ernie 3.5 | June 2023 |
| Ernie 4.0 | October 2023 |
| Ernie X1 | March 2025 |

Plus "Turbo" variants released 2024–2025.

## User adoption (verbatim)
- December 2023: 100 million users
- April 2024: 200 million users
- June 2024: 300 million users

## Regulatory context
Public release happened **two weeks after** China's [[China AI Regulation|generative AI regulations]] entered force (2023-08-15). The 2023-08-31 launch is a clear regulatory milestone — Baidu was the first major Chinese chatbot to receive public-launch approval.
