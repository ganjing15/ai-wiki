# Sam Altman

## Source
[Wikipedia: Sam Altman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sam_Altman) → raw excerpt at [wikipedia-people.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-people.md).

## Biographical
- Born: 1985-04-22 in **Chicago** (the prior version of this page said St. Louis; Wikipedia says Chicago)
- Education: Stanford University (two years, did not complete degree)
- Current role: CEO of OpenAI

## OpenAI tenure
- Co-founded OpenAI: 2015
- Became CEO: 2019
- Removed by board: **2023-11-17** — board cited that he "was not consistently candid in his communications"
- Reinstated: **2023-11-21**, after employee and investor backlash

## Y Combinator
- Joined as part-time partner: 2011
- President: from February 2014 (replacing Paul Graham)
- Relationship terminated: early 2020

## Other ventures (per [Wikipedia: Sam Altman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sam_Altman))
- **Worldcoin / Tools For Humanity** — co-founded 2019
- **Helion Energy** — chairman of the board
- **Hydrazine Capital** — co-founded 2012 with brother Jack
- **Reddit** — CEO for eight days in 2014; board member until 2022
