# Geoffrey Hinton

## Source
[Wikipedia: Geoffrey Hinton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geoffrey_Hinton) → raw excerpt at [wikipedia-people.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-people.md).

## Biographical
- Born: 1947-12-06 in London
- Education: King's College, Cambridge (BA experimental psychology, 1970); University of Edinburgh (PhD AI, 1978)
- Currently: University Professor Emeritus, University of Toronto (since 1987)

## 2018 ACM Turing Award (verbatim)
> "for conceptual and engineering breakthroughs that have made deep neural networks a critical component of computing."

Joint with **Yoshua Bengio and Yann LeCun**.

## 2024 Nobel Prize in Physics (verbatim)
> Jointly with John Hopfield "for foundational discoveries and inventions that enable machine learning with artificial neural networks."

Boltzmann machine explicitly cited.

## May 2023 Google resignation (verbatim)
> "I left so that I could talk about the dangers of AI without considering how this impacts Google."

## AI risk warnings (verbatim)
> AI could "wipe out humanity," and that this is "not inconceivable."

> Chatbots "can learn independently and share knowledge" and "whenever one copy acquires new information, it is automatically disseminated."
