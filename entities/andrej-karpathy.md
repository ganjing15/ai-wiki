## OpenAI (founding member)
- Research scientist 2015–2017
- Returned 2023-02-09
- Departed (second time) by 2024-02-13

## Tesla AI (verbatim)
> "In June 2017 he became Tesla's director of artificial intelligence and reported to Elon Musk."

Held until July 2022.

## Eureka Labs (verbatim)
> "On July 16, 2024, Karpathy announced on his X account that he started a new AI education company called Eureka Labs."

First product: "the AI course, LLM101n."

## Educational legacy
Authored Stanford's "CS 231n: Convolutional Neural Networks for Visual Recognition" — grew "from 150 students in 2015 to 750 in 2017." YouTube neural-network construction videos since Feb 2023.
