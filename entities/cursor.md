# Cursor (Anysphere)

## Source
[Wikipedia: Anysphere](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anysphere) → raw excerpt at [wikipedia-anysphere-cursor.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-anysphere-cursor.md).

## Founding (verbatim)
> "The company was incorporated in 2022 by Michael Truell, Sualeh Asif, Arvid Lunnemark and Aman Sanger while they were students at Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)."

## What Cursor is (verbatim)
> "Cursor is a fork of Visual Studio Code that integrates AI-assisted software development capabilities directly into the editor."

## Underlying models (verbatim)
> "The editor integrates generative models from Anthropic, OpenAI and others."

## Funding history (verbatim)
| Round | Date | Amount |

| Series A | 2024 | $60M |

## ARR (verbatim)
- January 2025: "crossed US$100 million in annual recurring revenue"
- June 2025: "topped US$500 million ARR"
- November 2025: "exceeding $1 billion annualized revenue"

## Headcount
~300 people (2025).

## Significance
Cursor's $1B ARR milestone in November 2025 makes it one of the fastest-growing software companies in history — going from $100M to $1B ARR in about 10 months.
