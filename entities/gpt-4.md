## Variants (verbatim)

- GPT-4 Turbo with Vision: part of **November 2023** release

## Context window (verbatim, [Wikipedia: GPT-4](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GPT-4))
> "The default version had a 8K context window, with a special version in the API supporting up to 32K tokens. GPT-4 Turbo features a 128K context window."

## Open-letter pause (verbatim)
> "Various AI researchers and tech executives, including Elon Musk, Steve Wozniak and AI researcher Yoshua Bengio, called for a six-month long pause for all LLMs stronger than GPT-4, citing existential risks and a potential AI singularity concerns in an open letter from the Future of Life Institute."

## Specific scores cited in Wikipedia (verbatim)
> "GPT-4 produced code vulnerable to SQL injection attacks 5% of the time, an improvement over GitHub Copilot from the year 2021, which produced vulnerabilities 40% of the time."

> "GPT-4 scored within the top 1% for originality and fluency" on the Torrance Tests.

## arXiv abstract claim (verbatim, [arXiv:2303.08774](https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.08774))
> "passing a simulated bar exam with a score around the top 10% of test takers"
> "human-level performance on various professional and academic benchmarks"
