# Inflection AI

## Founders (verbatim)
> "Reid Hoffman, Mustafa Suleyman and Karén Simonyan"

## Founded
2022.

## Pi (verbatim)
> "Pi, named for 'personal intelligence,' that is intended to function as an artificial intelligence-based personal assistant"

## March 2024 Microsoft acqui-hire (verbatim)
> "In March 2024, Suleyman and Simonyan announced their departure from the company in order to start Microsoft AI, with Microsoft acqui-hiring nearly the entirety of its 70-person workforce. As part of the deal, Microsoft paid Inflection $650 million to license its technology."

## Significance
The Inflection → Microsoft transaction was structured as a personnel + licensing deal rather than a traditional acquisition — likely to avoid antitrust scrutiny that would have applied to a Microsoft purchase of an AI lab. Microsoft got the team and tech rights; Inflection received **$650 million**. Often cited as the inaugural example of the "AI acqui-hire" pattern.

## Frontier AI Safety Commitments
Inflection AI is one of the **16 signatories** of the Frontier AI Safety Commitments at the AI Seoul Summit (2024-05-21) — see [seoul-frontier-commitments.md](../raw/articles/seoul-frontier-commitments.md).
