# Dario Amodei

## Source
[Wikipedia: Dario Amodei](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei) → raw excerpt at [wikipedia-people.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-people.md).

## Biographical
- Born: 1983 in San Francisco
- Education: Stanford (BS Physics); Princeton (MS, PhD Biophysics)
- Current role: CEO of Anthropic

## OpenAI tenure (verbatim)
> "Vice President of Research at OpenAI starting in 2016. He departed due to 'directional differences' with the organization."

## Anthropic founding (verbatim)
> "Founded Anthropic in 2021 with his sister Daniela and other former OpenAI senior members. He serves as CEO."

For Anthropic funding rounds and headcount, see [[Anthropic]] (sourced to [Wikipedia: Anthropic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anthropic)).

## "Machines of Loving Grace" (verbatim, October 2024)
> "I think that most people are underestimating just how radical the upside of AI could be, just as I think most people are underestimating how bad the risks could be."

## Policy positions (verbatim summary)
> Advocates an "entente" strategy wherein democracies use advanced AI for military advantage while sharing benefits among cooperating nations. Recent essays focus on AI risks including misalignment, biological weapons proliferation, and economic disruption.
