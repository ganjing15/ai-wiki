# Greg Brockman

## Source
[Wikipedia: Greg Brockman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greg_Brockman) → raw excerpt at [wikipedia-greg-brockman.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-greg-brockman.md).

## Background (verbatim)
> "Enrolled at Harvard University but left a year later, briefly enrolling at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology."

Stripe: joined 2010; "became Stripe's first CTO" in 2013; departed May 2015.

## OpenAI (verbatim)
> "Co-founded OpenAI in December 2015 alongside Altman, Sutskever and others"

Roles: CTO; later **President**.

## November 2023 events (verbatim)
> "On November 17, 2023, alongside the firing of Sam Altman, Brockman was told he had been removed from the board."

> "Announced that he had quit the company" the same day.

He returned with Altman after the board reversal on November 21.

## 2024 sabbatical (verbatim)
> "Brockman took a sabbatical from August to November 2024" and subsequently returned.

## Current
President of OpenAI.
