# Manus (Butterfly Effect AI agent)

## Source
[Wikipedia: Manus (AI agent)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manus_(AI_agent)) → raw excerpt at [wikipedia-manus.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-manus.md).

## Developer
**Butterfly Effect Pte. Ltd.** — founded 2022 by **Xiao Hong** (two months before ChatGPT's public launch). Originally Beijing/Wuhan; relocated to Singapore mid-2025.

## Co-founders
- **Xiao Hong** — founder
- **Ji Yichao** ("Peak") — co-founder, Chief Scientist; born 1992; previously founded Peak Labs (2012); Forbes China 30 Under 30 (2012, 2013)
- **Zhang Tao** — Product Director

## Launch impact (Wikipedia verbatim)
> Demo video showing autonomous task completion (resume screening, stock analysis) garnered "more than one million views within twenty hours."

Secondary-market invitation codes sold for ¥50,000–¥100,000 (~$7,000–$13,800) on Chinese platforms.

## Earlier product (Monica)
In 2023, Butterfly Effect released Monica — a ChatGPT-powered browser extension for translation, summarization, and writing.

## Revenue
- August 2025: ~$90M run rate
- December 2025: $125M run rate

## US regulatory review (May 2025)
US Treasury reviewed Benchmark's investment under the Outbound Investment Security Program (OISP, effective 2025-01-02). Benchmark's counsel argued Manus "did not train its own foundation models" and Butterfly Effect was Cayman Islands–incorporated. Scrutiny diminished after Singapore relocation.

## Singapore relocation (mid-2025)
Following Series B: HQ moved from Wuhan/Beijing to Singapore. ~40 of ~120 China-based employees relocated; remainder laid off. By late 2025: ~100 employees, primarily Singapore-based. Closed Chinese-language social media; blocked mainland China access; shelved planned Chinese-language version (Alibaba Qwen partnership).

## Meta acquisition (December 2025)
- **Announced**: December 2025
- **Terms**: Undisclosed; reportedly $2–3 billion
- **Context**: Manus was seeking Series C at ~$2B valuation
- **Plans**: Integration into Meta AI; Manus app/site to continue standalone

## Chinese government block (April 2026)
- **January 2026**: China's Ministry of Commerce announced "evaluative investigation" regarding export controls, technology transfer, foreign investment compliance
- **March 2026**: Exit bans issued for Manus executives under scrutiny
- **2026-04-27**: **National Development and Reform Commission blocked the acquisition**, requiring parties to "withdraw the acquisition transaction"
- **Meta response**: Transaction "complied with applicable law"; expected "an appropriate resolution to the inquiry"

## Significance
- One of the first general-purpose autonomous AI agents to achieve mainstream attention (the "DeepSeek moment" for agents in March 2025)
- First Chinese-origin AI company to be blocked by Chinese authorities from accepting a US acquisition, signaling a new chapter in US/China AI tech competition
- Notable that Wikipedia explicitly states Manus "did not train its own foundation models" — it is an orchestration layer over external LLMs (third-party reporting suggests Claude 3.5 Sonnet + fine-tuned Qwen)
