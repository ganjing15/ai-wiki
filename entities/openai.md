## Microsoft investment (verbatim, Wikipedia OpenAI)
> "Microsoft previously invested over $13 billion into OpenAI" with an additional "$10 billion investment in OpenAI vested over multiple years" announced January 23, 2023.

> "Following the 2025 restructuring, Microsoft owned a 27% stake in the for-profit OpenAI Group PBC, valued at $135 billion."

## November 2023 governance crisis (verbatim, Wikipedia OpenAI)
> "On November 17, 2023, Sam Altman was removed as CEO" by the board citing lack of confidence.
> "On November 21, 2023, after continued negotiations, Altman and Brockman returned to the company in their prior roles" with a reconstructed board.
