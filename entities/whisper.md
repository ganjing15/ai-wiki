# Whisper (OpenAI speech recognition)

## Source
[Wikipedia: Whisper](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Whisper_(speech_recognition_system)) → raw excerpt at [wikipedia-whisper.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-whisper.md).

## Developer
OpenAI.

## Release
**2022-09-21** — exactly two months before ChatGPT (2022-11-30).

## License
**MIT License** — fully open-source.

## Architecture (verbatim)
> "encoder-decoder transformer architecture"

## Capabilities (verbatim)
> "transcribing speech in English and multiple other languages, and can translate several non-English languages into English"

## Significance
One of OpenAI's earliest production-quality open-weight releases. Whisper sits between OpenAI's pre-ChatGPT image work (DALL-E 2, April 2022) and the ChatGPT launch — in retrospect, the September 2022 release is part of the immediate run-up to the AI mainstream moment. The MIT license is also notable: post-ChatGPT OpenAI has trended sharply away from open releases.
