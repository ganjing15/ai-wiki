# "Attention Is All You Need" — Vaswani et al. 2017
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Attention_Is_All_You_Need
Direct paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762
Fetched: 2026-05-07

## Authors (verbatim)
"Ashish Vaswani, Noam Shazeer, Niki Parmar, Jakob Uszkoreit, Llion Jones, Aidan Gomez, Łukasz Kaiser, and Illia Polosukhin"

## Publication (verbatim)
"June 12, 2017" at "the 31st Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems (NIPS)"

## Architecture introduced (verbatim)
"Introduced a new deep learning architecture known as the transformer, based on the attention mechanism."
Specifically: "scaled dot-product attention and self-attention," "multi-head attention," and "positional encoding" mechanisms.

## Citation count (verbatim)
"As of 2025, the paper has been cited more than 173,000 times, placing it among the top ten most-cited papers of the 21st century."

## Authors' diaspora (verbatim)
"After the paper was published by Google, each of the authors left the company to join other companies or to found startups."

(Notable: Aidan Gomez co-founded [[Cohere]] in 2019. Noam Shazeer founded Character.AI; later returned to Google. Niki Parmar joined Adept; later Anthropic. The diaspora pattern is now widely cited as evidence that frontier AI capability concentrates with the people, not the institution.)
