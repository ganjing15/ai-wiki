## Title
"Frontier AI Safety Commitments, AI Seoul Summit 2024"

## Summit dates
2024-05-21 to 2024-05-22

## Signatories (verbatim, 16 organizations)
Amazon, Anthropic, Cohere, Google, G42, IBM, Inflection AI, Meta, Microsoft, Mistral AI, Naver, OpenAI, Samsung Electronics, Technology Innovation Institute, xAI, Zhipu.ai

## Key commitments (verbatim)
**Outcome 1**: Organizations must "assess the risks posed by their frontier models or systems across the AI lifecycle" and "set out thresholds at which severe risks posed by a model or system, unless adequately mitigated, would be deemed intolerable."

They commit to "articulate how risk mitigations will be identified and implemented to keep risks within defined thresholds" and pledge not to deploy models "if mitigations cannot be applied to keep risks below the thresholds."
