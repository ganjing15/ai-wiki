# Wikipedia: Pause Giant AI Experiments — An Open Letter
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pause_Giant_AI_Experiments:_An_Open_Letter
Fetched: 2026-05-07

## Publication (verbatim)
"Published by the Future of Life Institute in March 2023."

## Core request (verbatim)
"All AI labs to immediately pause for at least 6 months the training of AI systems more powerful than GPT-4."

## Signatories (verbatim)
"It received more than 30,000 signatures, including academic AI researchers and industry CEOs such as Yoshua Bengio, Stuart Russell, Elon Musk, Steve Wozniak and Yuval Noah Harari."

## Sam Altman response (verbatim)
The letter was "missing most technical nuance about where we need the pause."
He clarified: "An earlier version of the letter claimed OpenAI is training GPT-5 right now. We are not and won't for some time."

## Bill Gates response (verbatim)
Declined to sign — does not think "asking one particular group to pause solves the challenges."

## Outcome (verbatim)
"As of July 2024, a pause has not been realized."
GPT-5 was eventually announced in 2025.
