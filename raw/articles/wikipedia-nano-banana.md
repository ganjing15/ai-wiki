# Nano Banana — Wikipedia excerpt

> Wikipedia note: The URL https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nano_Banana redirects to the Gemini (language model) article; the Nano Banana section is embedded within that article.

## Infobox (Gemini language model page)
- Developer: Google AI and Google DeepMind
- Multimodal capabilities: text, images, audio, video, code
- Current stable releases include Gemini 3.1 Pro, 3 Deep Think, 3 Flash, 3.1 Flash Lite (as of March 3, 2026)

## Nano Banana section (verbatim from Wikipedia / WebFetch)
> "The Gemini 2.5 Flash Image model (codenamed 'Nano Banana') became viral in August 2025, generating 'over 10 million' new Gemini app users and facilitating '200 million image edits within weeks.'"

## Release timeline (from Wikipedia + Google DeepMind pages)
- **August 12, 2025**: First appeared anonymously on AI evaluation platform Arena
- **August 26, 2025**: Public release via the Gemini app — official model name Gemini 2.5 Flash Image
- **November 20, 2025**: Nano Banana Pro launched — official model name Gemini 3 Pro Image; improved text rendering and world knowledge
- **February 26, 2026**: Nano Banana 2 released — official model name Gemini 3.1 Flash Image

## Nickname origin (Wikipedia)
> "nicknames given to Naina Raisinghani, Product Manager at Google DeepMind"

## Capabilities (Wikipedia + Google blog)
- Change hairstyles, backdrops, and mix photos with natural language cues
- Maintains subject consistency across image revisions
- Multi-image fusion
- SynthID watermarking to identify AI-generated content
- Nano Banana 2: subject consistency for up to 5 characters and 14 objects; precision text rendering; 512px to 4K resolution output
- Nano Banana 2: SynthID + C2PA Content Credentials

## Nano Banana 2 verbatim quotes (from blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/ai/nano-banana-2/)
> "Now you can get the advanced world knowledge, quality and reasoning you love in Nano Banana Pro, at lightning-fast speed."
> "The model pulls from Gemini's real-world knowledge base, and is powered by real-time information and images from web search"
> "Nano Banana 2 delivers vibrant lighting, richer textures and sharper details"

## Impact
- Became a "viral Internet sensation on social media, particularly for its photorealistic '3D figurine' images"
- Attracted over 10 million new Gemini app users
- Facilitated more than 200 million image edits within weeks of launch
