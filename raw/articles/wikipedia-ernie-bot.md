# Wikipedia: Ernie Bot
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernie_Bot
Fetched: 2026-05-06

## Developer
Baidu.

## Launch (verbatim)
- Invited testing: 2023-03-16
- Public release: 2023-08-31, "after receiving approval from Chinese regulators"

## Model versions
- Ernie 3.0 — initial launch
- Ernie 3.5 — June 2023
- Ernie 4.0 — October 2023
- Ernie X1 — March 2025
- Plus "Turbo" variants released 2024–2025

## User adoption (verbatim)
- 100 million users by December 2023
- 200 million by April 2024
- 300 million by June 2024

## Note
Public release happened **two weeks after** China's generative AI regulations entered force (2023-08-15). See [[China AI Regulation]] and [wikipedia-china-ai-regulation.md](wikipedia-china-ai-regulation.md).
