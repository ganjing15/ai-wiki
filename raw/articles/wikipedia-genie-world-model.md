# Genie (world model) — Wikipedia excerpt

## What it is
Genie is a series of AI-powered world models developed by Google DeepMind that generate interactive, game-like virtual environments from text, images, or sketches.

## Developer
Google DeepMind

## Release timeline
- **March 2024**: Original Genie — 2D environments only
- **December 2024**: Genie 2 — added 3D capability
- **August 2025**: Genie 3 — improved resolution and memory
- **January 29, 2026**: Project Genie (public access) launched

## Capabilities
- Generates interactive 3D environments in 720p at 24 frames per second
- Creates worlds from user input and previous frames
- Trained on thousands of hours of video game footage
- Memory capacity: approximately one minute (Genie 3)
- Converts dashcam or phone videos into usable interactive worlds

## Key quoted statement
> "Genie 3 is capable of generating and modifying interactive worlds in real-time," with enhanced memory and resolution compared to its predecessors.

## Access (as of 2026-01-29)
- Project Genie restricted to Google AI Ultra subscribers ($250/month)
- United States only; ages 18+
- 60-second exploration limit per session

## Notable adoption
- **Waymo World Model** (February 2026): specialized derivative for autonomous vehicle training; simulates edge case scenarios including tornadoes and unusual pedestrian behavior
