# Wikipedia: Sora (text-to-video model)
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sora_(text-to-video_model)
Fetched: 2026-04-30

## Release dates
- Developer: OpenAI
- Demo / preview clips: 2024-02-15
- Public availability (ChatGPT Plus/Pro, US and Canada): 2024-12-09

## Architecture (verbatim)
"Sora is a diffusion transformer, a denoising latent diffusion model with one transformer as its denoiser."

## Capabilities (verbatim)
OpenAI stated the system could "generate videos as long as one minute."

## Limitations (verbatim)
OpenAI acknowledged "limited capacity to simulate complex physics, to understand causality and to differentiate left from right."

## Copyright (verbatim)
"By default, the generator used copyrighted material in its videos, unless copyright holders actively opt out."

## Watermarks (verbatim)
"Third-party programs that could remove the watermark from Sora 2 videos had become prevalent."

## Sora 2
"Sora 2 was released to select users in the US and Canada at the end of September 2025" and "integrated social media features into the app."

## Discontinuation
"On March 24, 2026, OpenAI announced that the Sora app and API would be shutting down."
