## Title (verbatim)
"Winning the AI Race: America's AI Action Plan"

## Date
2025-07-23

## Scope (verbatim)
Over 90 federal policy actions.

## Three pillars (verbatim)
1. Accelerating Innovation
2. Building American AI Infrastructure
3. Leading in International Diplomacy and Security

## Cross-cutting priorities (verbatim summary)
- Exporting American AI to allies
- Expediting data center permits
- Removing regulatory barriers to innovation
- Ensuring frontier AI models remain "objective and free from ideological bias"

## Specific actions cited
- Commerce and State Departments to deliver "secure, full-stack AI export packages — including hardware, models, software, applications, and standards — to America's friends and allies around the world"
- Updated Federal procurement guidelines so that the government only contracts with frontier LLM developers ensuring systems are "objective and free from top-down ideological bias"

## Quotes from officials (verbatim)
- **Michael Kratsios** (OSTP Director): "This plan galvanizes Federal efforts to turbocharge our innovation capacity, build cutting-edge infrastructure, and lead globally."
- **David Sacks** (AI and Crypto Czar): "To win the AI race, the U.S. must lead in innovation, infrastructure, and global partnerships."
- **Marco Rubio** (Secretary of State): "Winning the AI Race is non-negotiable. America must continue to be the dominant force in artificial intelligence."
