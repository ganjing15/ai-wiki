# Seedance 2.0 — ByteDance SEED Lab official launch blog

## Release date
February 12, 2026

## Developer
ByteDance SEED Lab

## Architecture
Unified multimodal audio-video joint generation architecture, trained on integrated audio-visual framework supporting mixed-modality inputs.

## Supported input modalities
- Text (natural language instructions)
- Images (up to 9)
- Video clips (up to 3)
- Audio clips (up to 3)

## Output capabilities
- 15-second high-quality multi-shot audio-video generation
- Dual-channel stereo audio
- Multi-track parallel output: background music, ambient effects, voiceovers

## Verbatim quotes from ByteDance
> "supports stable and controllable video extension and editing, enabling regular users to command the video creation process."

## Key capabilities
- Complex motion and interaction rendering with physical accuracy
- Multimodal reference generation (composition, motion, camera movement, visual effects)
- Video editing with targeted modifications to clips, characters, actions
- Prompt-driven camera planning and automatic visual composition
- Natural foley sound design with subtle acoustic details
- Character consistency: preserves facial features, clothing, and style through 360° head turns and dramatic lighting changes

## Variants
- **Seedance 2.0** — full quality
- **Seedance 2.0 Fast** — reduced latency variant

## Access
- Consumer web apps
- CapCut mobile/desktop editor
- Programmatic API (fal.ai, Replicate, etc.)

## Improvements over Seedance 1.5
- Enhanced motion stability and physical realism
- Improved instruction-following accuracy
- Superior audio-visual synchronization
- Better performance in complex multi-subject scenarios

## Known limitations (at launch)
- Detail stability refinement needed
- Occasional audio distortion
- Multi-subject consistency optimization pending
- Text rendering accuracy improvements pending
