# Wikipedia: ChatGPT Atlas
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ChatGPT_Atlas
Fetched: 2026-05-06

## Launch (verbatim)
"On October 21, 2025, OpenAI announced ChatGPT Atlas and released it for macOS."

## What it is (verbatim)
"ChatGPT Atlas is an AI browser developed by OpenAI."

## Architecture (verbatim)
"Built on the Chromium open source project," integrates ChatGPT through a sidebar assistant within the browsing interface.

## Capabilities (verbatim)
- "summarize page content, compare products, and analyze information from websites"
- Paid users: "agent mode" allowing ChatGPT to interact with websites to complete tasks
- "cursor chat" for rewriting selected text inline

## Competitive context (verbatim)
"The initial macOS rollout places the browser in a position to compete with established browsers like Google Chrome, Safari, and Microsoft Edge."
Atlas arrived "shortly after the release of Comet by Perplexity AI."
