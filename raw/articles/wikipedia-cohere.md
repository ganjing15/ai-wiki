# Wikipedia: Cohere
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cohere
Fetched: 2026-05-06

## Founding (verbatim)
"Cohere was founded in 2019 by Aidan Gomez, Ivan Zhang, and Nick Frosst" — headquartered in Toronto.

## Offices
"Toronto, Ontario, Canada" plus Montreal, NYC, San Francisco, London, Paris, Seoul.

## Models
Command A, Command A Reasoning, Command A Vision, Cohere North, Aya Vision.
Specializes in "large language models and generative AI for regulated industries."

## Funding (verbatim)
- 2025: "$500 million at a $6.8 billion valuation"
- Follow-on: "$100 million" raising valuation to ~$7B
- February 2026 revenue: "Cohere's revenue as $240 million"

## Positioning (verbatim)
"An alternative to OpenAI" focused on enterprise; "finance, healthcare, manufacturing, and energy fields, as well as the public sector."
Partnerships: Oracle, Salesforce.

## Cross-ref
Cohere is one of the **16 signatories** of the Frontier AI Safety Commitments (AI Seoul Summit 2024) — see [seoul-frontier-commitments.md](seoul-frontier-commitments.md).
