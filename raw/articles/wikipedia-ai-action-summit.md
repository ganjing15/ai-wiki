# Wikipedia: AI Action Summit (Paris)
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AI_Action_Summit
Fetched: 2026-05-06

## Dates and location (verbatim)
"From 10 to 11 February 2025" at "Grand Palais in Paris, France."

## Declaration (verbatim)
"58 countries signed the 'Statement on Inclusive and Sustainable Artificial Intelligence for People and the Planet,' which outlined principles covering accessibility, ethical AI development, innovation encouragement, labor market impacts, environmental sustainability, and international cooperation."

## US/UK refusal (verbatim)
"The US and UK refused to sign the declaration on inclusive and sustainable AI."
The UK stated the agreement "did not go far enough in defining global governance of AI and addressing concerns about its impact on national security."

Armenia, Australia, Austria, Belgium, Brazil, Bulgaria, Cambodia, Canada, Chile, China, Croatia, Cyprus, Czechia, Denmark, Djibouti, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, India, Indonesia, Ireland, Italy, Japan, Kazakhstan, Kenya, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Mexico, Monaco, Morocco, New Zealand, Nigeria, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Rwanda, Senegal, Serbia, Singapore, Slovakia, Slovenia, South Africa, South Korea, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Thailand, Netherlands, United Arab Emirates, Ukraine, Uruguay, Vatican.
