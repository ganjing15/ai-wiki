# Manus (AI agent) — Wikipedia excerpt

## Infobox
- **Name:** Manus (Latin: "hand")
- **Type:** Autonomous AI agent
- **Developer:** Butterfly Effect Pte. Ltd.
- **Founder:** Xiao Hong
- **Initial release:** 2025-03-06
- **Stable release:** Manus 1.6 (2025-12-15)
- **License:** Proprietary
- **Website:** manus.im

## Founding
Butterfly Effect was established in 2022 by Xiao Hong, two months before ChatGPT's public launch. Initial offices in Beijing and Wuhan; targeted North America, Japan, and South Korea.

## Key team
- **Ji Yichao** ("Peak") — co-founder, Chief Scientist. Born 1992; raised in Colorado and Beijing. Previously founded Peak Labs (2012) with ZhenFund and HSG backing. Named to Forbes China 30 Under 30 (2012, 2013).
- **Zhang Tao** — Product Director

## Product history
- **2023**: Released Monica, a ChatGPT-powered browser extension (translation, summarization, writing)
- **October 2024**: Began Manus development, drawing inspiration from Cursor (San Francisco AI coding tool)
- **2025-03-06**: Beta launch with invitation-only access

## Launch impact (verbatim)
> Demo video showing autonomous task completion (resume screening, stock analysis) garnered "more than one million views within twenty hours."

Secondary-market invitation codes sold for ¥50,000–¥100,000 (~$7,000–$13,800) on Chinese platforms; WSJ reported codes exceeded $1,000.

## Underlying technology
Wikipedia article does not specify which foundation models power Manus. (Third-party reporting indicates Anthropic Claude 3.5 Sonnet and fine-tuned Alibaba Qwen variants, but Wikipedia does not confirm this.)

Series B valuation ~$500M; Chetan Puttagunta joined board. Other investors: Wang Huiwen (Meituan co-founder), Old Friendship Capital, ZhenFund, Tencent, HSG.

## US regulatory review
In May 2025, the US Treasury reviewed Benchmark's investment under the Outbound Investment Security Program (OISP, effective 2025-01-02). Benchmark's counsel argued the investment fell outside OISP because Manus "did not train its own foundation models" and Butterfly Effect was Cayman Islands–incorporated. Scrutiny diminished after Singapore relocation.

## Market response
- **December 2025**: Revenue run rate increased from ~$90M (August) to $125M

## Relocation to Singapore (mid-2025)
Headquarters moved from Wuhan and Beijing to Singapore. ~40 of ~120 China-based employees relocated; remainder laid off. By late 2025, ~100 employees, primarily Singapore-based. Closed Chinese-language social media; blocked mainland China access; shelved Chinese-language version (Alibaba Qwen partnership).

## Meta acquisition
- **Announced**: December 2025
- **Terms**: Undisclosed; reportedly $2–3B
- **Context**: Manus was seeking Series C at ~$2B valuation
- **Plans**: Integration into Meta AI; Manus app/site to continue standalone
- **HQ**: Remained in Singapore

## Chinese government review and block
- **January 2026**: China's Ministry of Commerce announced "evaluative investigation" regarding export controls, technology transfer, foreign-investment compliance
- **March 2026**: Exit bans issued for Manus executives under scrutiny
- **2026-04-27**: National Development and Reform Commission **blocked** the acquisition, requiring parties to "withdraw the acquisition transaction"
- **Meta response**: Transaction "complied with applicable law"; anticipated "an appropriate resolution to the inquiry"
