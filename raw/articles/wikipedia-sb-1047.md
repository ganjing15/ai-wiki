# Wikipedia: SB 1047 (California)
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SB_1047
Fetched: 2026-05-07

## Full name
"Safe and Secure Innovation for Frontier Artificial Intelligence Models Act"

## Author and date
California State Senator Scott Wiener.
Introduced: 2024-02-07.

## Coverage thresholds (verbatim)
- "Trained using a quantity of computing power greater than 10^26 integer or floating-point operations"
- "Cost more than $100 million to train"
- Fine-tuned models using over $10 million also covered.

## Newsom veto (verbatim)
Vetoed by Governor Gavin Newsom on **2024-09-29**.
Newsom's rationale: the regulatory framework was "based only on computational size" without considering whether systems operated in high-risk environments.

## Lab responses (verbatim)
- **Anthropic**: Dario Amodei said the amended version was "substantially improved" and believed "its benefits likely outweigh its costs"
- **OpenAI**: opposed; claimed it would "hurt innovation"
- **Google, Microsoft, Anthropic**: "proposed substantial amendments"
