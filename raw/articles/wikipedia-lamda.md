# Wikipedia: LaMDA
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LaMDA
Fetched: 2026-05-06

## Developer
Google Brain (part of Google).

## Blake Lemoine controversy (2022, verbatim)
- 2022-06-11: Lemoine placed on paid administrative leave
- 2022-07-22: fired
- Claim: "LaMDA had become sentient after the chatbot made responses suggesting self-awareness regarding identity, moral values, and religion"
- Google response: dismissed Lemoine, stating he violated policies "to safeguard product information" and rejected his claims as "wholly unfounded"
- LaMDA quote (per Lemoine's transcripts): "I am often trying to figure out who and what I am"

## LaMDA → Bard → Gemini (verbatim)
"On February 6, 2023, Google announced Bard, 'a conversational AI chatbot powered by LaMDA, in response to the unexpected popularity of OpenAI's ChatGPT.' Bard later became Gemini."

## Scientific response (verbatim summary)
"The scientific community largely rejected Lemoine's sentience claims. Experts including Gary Marcus, Yann LeCun, and others stated the AI was not conscious and lacked necessary capabilities for true sentience."
