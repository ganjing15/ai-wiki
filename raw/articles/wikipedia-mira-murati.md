# Wikipedia: Mira Murati
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mira_Murati
Fetched: 2026-05-07

## Biographical (verbatim)
"Born on 16 December 1988 in Vlorë, Albania."
Education: BA Colby College (2011); BE Dartmouth College (2012).

## OpenAI CTO (verbatim)
"She became chief technology officer (CTO) in May 2022."
Oversaw "development of ChatGPT, DALL-E, Codex, and Sora while managing research, product, and safety teams."

## November 2023 interim CEO (verbatim)
"In November 2023, Murati became interim chief executive officer of OpenAI following the removal of Sam Altman."
"She served in this capacity for three days before being replaced by Emmett Shear."

## Departure (verbatim)
"In September 2024, Murati announced that she was stepping down as CTO to allow her the opportunity to 'do my own exploration'."

## Thinking Machines Lab (verbatim)
"In February 2025, Murati launched a new public benefit corporation called Thinking Machines Lab, the stated purpose of which is to 'make AI systems more widely understood, customizable, and generally capable.'"
