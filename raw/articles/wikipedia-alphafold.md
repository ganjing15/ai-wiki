# Wikipedia: AlphaFold
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AlphaFold
Fetched: 2026-04-30

## Developer
"AlphaFold is an artificial intelligence (AI) program developed by DeepMind, a subsidiary of Alphabet."

## Versions
- AlphaFold 1: 2018
- AlphaFold 2: 2020
- AlphaFold 3: announced 2024-05-08

## AlphaFold 3 (verbatim)
Predicts structures of "protein complexes with DNA, RNA, post-translational modifications and selected ligands and ions."

## Nature publication (verbatim)
"On 15 July 2021, the AlphaFold 2 paper was published in Nature as an advance access publication alongside open source software and a searchable database of species proteomes."

## 2024 Nobel Prize (verbatim)
"Demis Hassabis and John Jumper shared one half of the 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry, awarded 'for protein structure prediction,' while the other half went to David Baker 'for computational protein design.'"
