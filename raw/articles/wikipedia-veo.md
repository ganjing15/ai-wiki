# Wikipedia: Veo (text-to-video model)
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Veo_(text-to-video_model)
Fetched: 2026-05-06

## Developer
Google DeepMind.

## Initial release
2024-05.

## Original Veo capabilities (verbatim)
"Google claimed that it could generate 1080p videos over a minute long."

## Veo 2 (verbatim)
"In December 2024, Google released Veo 2, available via VideoFX. It supports 4K resolution video generation and has an improved understanding of physics."

## Veo 3 (verbatim)
"In May 2025, Google released Veo 3, which not only generates videos but also creates synchronized audio — including dialogue, sound effects, and ambient noise — to match the visuals."

## Current stable
Veo 3.1, 2025-10-15.

## Products
"Google also announced Flow, a video-creation tool powered by Veo and Imagen."
