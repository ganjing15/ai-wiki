# Wikipedia: Regulation of artificial intelligence (China section)
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regulation_of_artificial_intelligence#China
Fetched: 2026-04-30

## August 2023 Generative AI measures (verbatim)
"On August 15, 2023, China's first generative AI measures officially came into force, becoming one of the first comprehensive national regulatory frameworks for generative AI."

Apply broadly to all providers offering generative AI services to Chinese citizens, including foreign companies. Establish requirements for "data protection, transparency, and algorithmic accountability."

## 2025 Content labeling (verbatim)
"In 2025, Chinese regulators issued the Measures for Labeling of AI-Generated Synthetic Content."
Mandates disclosure labels and embedded metadata. Online service providers must reveal identity and detect synthetic content with "conspicuous consumer disclosures."

## Earlier regulations (verbatim)
- Algorithm Recommendation Provisions (March 2022)
- Deep Synthesis Provisions (January 2023)

Both shape governance "including requirements for watermarking and algorithm filing."

## Cyberspace Administration of China (verbatim)
"As of mid-2024, over 1,400 AI algorithms had been already registered under the CAC's algorithm filing regime," with disclosure requirements and penalties.
