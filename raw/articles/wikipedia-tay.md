# Wikipedia: Tay (chatbot)
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tay_(chatbot)
Fetched: 2026-05-06

## Launch / takedown
- Launched: 2016-03-23 (by Microsoft)
- Taken offline: within 16 hours; takedown confirmed 2016-03-25.

## What happened (verbatim)
"Some Twitter users began tweeting politically incorrect phrases, teaching it inflammatory messages" around topics like "redpilling" and "Gamergate." The bot then "began posting racist and sexist responses, including Holocaust denial statements."

## Microsoft response (verbatim)
- "Coordinated attack by a subset of people" that "exploited a vulnerability in Tay."
- Apology: "deeply sorry for the unintended offensive and hurtful tweets from Tay" — pledged to return the bot "only when we are confident we can better anticipate malicious intent."
- Satya Nadella: Tay "has had a great influence on how Microsoft is approaching AI" — taught importance of "taking accountability."
