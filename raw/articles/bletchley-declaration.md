# UK Government — The Bletchley Declaration
Source: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/ai-safety-summit-2023-the-bletchley-declaration/the-bletchley-declaration-by-countries-attending-the-ai-safety-summit-1-2-november-2023
Fetched: 2026-04-30

## Title (verbatim)
"The Bletchley Declaration by Countries Attending the AI Safety Summit, 1-2 November 2023"

## Date
1–2 November 2023

## 28 Signatory countries (verbatim list)
Australia, Brazil, Canada, Chile, China, European Union, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Ireland, Israel, Italy, Japan, Kenya, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Netherlands, Nigeria, The Philippines, Republic of Korea, Rwanda, Singapore, Spain, Switzerland, Türkiye, Ukraine, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United States of America.

(New Zealand joined on 23 October 2024.)

## Key principles (verbatim)
"AI should be designed, developed, deployed, and used...in a manner that is safe, in such a way as to be human-centric, trustworthy and responsible"

International cooperation on AI safety addressing "human rights, transparency and explainability, fairness, accountability...safety"

Special focus on frontier AI risks with "potential for serious, even catastrophic, harm"

## Specific commitments (verbatim)
- "an internationally inclusive network of scientific research on frontier AI safety"
- "sustain an inclusive global dialogue"
- "continue research on frontier AI safety"
- "continue meeting" — another summit planned for 2024
