# NIST — Center for AI Standards and Innovation (CAISI)
Source: https://www.nist.gov/aisi
Fetched: 2026-05-06

## Full name
"Center for AI Standards and Innovation (CAISI)"

## Parent
NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology), under the US Department of Commerce.

## Mission (verbatim)
CAISI "will serve as industry's primary point of contact within the U.S. government to facilitate testing and collaborative research related to harnessing and securing the potential of commercial AI systems."

## Functions (verbatim summary)
- Developing guidelines for AI security
- Establishing voluntary agreements with AI developers
- Leading evaluations of AI capabilities and vulnerabilities
- Coordinating with federal agencies
- Representing US interests in international AI standards development
