# Wikipedia: Executive Order 14110
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Executive_Order_14110
Fetched: 2026-04-30

## Identification (verbatim)
Full name: "Executive Order on Safe, Secure, and Trustworthy Development and Use of Artificial Intelligence"
Signed: 2023-10-30 by President Joe Biden.

## Required actions (verbatim)
- Federal agencies create chief AI officer positions
- Department of Commerce's NIST: develop generative AI resources
- Department of Homeland Security: AI security guidelines and critical infrastructure coordination
- General Services Administration, Department of Education, National Science Foundation: appointed chief AI officers
- Department of Veterans Affairs: AI technology competition for healthcare worker burnout

## Revocation (verbatim)
"Hours after his inauguration as the 47th president of the United States, Donald Trump rescinded the order" on 2025-01-20.
