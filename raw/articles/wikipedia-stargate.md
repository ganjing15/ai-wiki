# Wikipedia: Stargate LLC
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stargate_LLC
Fetched: 2026-05-06

## Announcement (verbatim)
"January 21, 2025" — at a White House press conference.

## Participants (verbatim)
"OpenAI, SoftBank, Oracle Corporation, and investment firm MGX"

## Investment commitment (verbatim)
"up to US$500 billion in AI infrastructure in the United States by 2029"
Initial commitment: "$100 billion"

## Trump quote (verbatim)
"President Donald Trump announced the venture at a White House press conference" and called it "the largest AI infrastructure project in history."
He indicated "plans to use emergency declarations to expedite the project's development."

## Infrastructure (verbatim)
Initial: "10 data centers being built in Abilene, Texas"
Expansion: "more states and countries, like the United Kingdom, Norway, Japan and the United Arab Emirates"
By September 2025: "five additional data center sites across Texas, New Mexico, Ohio, and an undisclosed Midwestern location, targeting nearly 7 gigawatts of planned capacity."
