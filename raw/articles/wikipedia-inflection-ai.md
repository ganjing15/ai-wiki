## Founders (verbatim)
"Reid Hoffman, Mustafa Suleyman and Karén Simonyan"

## Founded
2022.

## Pi (verbatim)
"Pi, named for 'personal intelligence,' that is intended to function as an artificial intelligence-based personal assistant"

## March 2024 Microsoft acqui-hire (verbatim)
"In March 2024, Suleyman and Simonyan announced their departure from the company in order to start Microsoft AI, with Microsoft acqui-hiring nearly the entirety of its 70-person workforce. As part of the deal, Microsoft paid Inflection $650 million to license its technology."

## Significance
The Inflection → Microsoft transaction (March 2024) was a notable structure: not a traditional acquisition (which would face antitrust scrutiny) but a personnel + licensing deal that gave Microsoft most of Inflection's team plus tech rights for **$650 million**.
