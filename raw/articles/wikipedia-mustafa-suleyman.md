# Wikipedia: Mustafa Suleyman
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mustafa_Suleyman
Fetched: 2026-05-07

## Biographical (verbatim)
Born 1984 in London, England.

## DeepMind co-founder (verbatim)
"In 2010 Suleyman co-founded DeepMind Technologies, an artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning company, and became its chief product officer."

## Inflection AI (verbatim)
"In March 2022, Suleyman co-founded Inflection AI, a new AI lab venture with Greylock's Reid Hoffman."

## Microsoft AI (verbatim)
"In March 2024, Microsoft appointed Suleyman as Executive Vice President (EVP) and CEO of its newly created consumer AI unit, Microsoft AI."

## Timeline summary
- 2010: co-founded DeepMind (with Hassabis and Legg)

- 2022-03: co-founded Inflection AI
