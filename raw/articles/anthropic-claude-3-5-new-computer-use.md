## Computer Use feature (verbatim)

## SWE-bench Verified (verbatim)

## Pre-deployment testing (verbatim)
"joint pre-deployment testing of the new Claude 3.5 Sonnet model was conducted by the US AI Safety Institute (US AISI) and the UK Safety Institute (UK AISI)"

## Caveats (verbatim)
