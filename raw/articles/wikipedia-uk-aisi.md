# Wikipedia: AI Safety Institute (United Kingdom)
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AI_Safety_Institute_(United_Kingdom)
Fetched: 2026-04-30

## UK AISI
- Founded: November 2023 (evolved from Frontier AI Taskforce, April 2023)
- Parent: Department for Science, Innovation and Technology (DSIT)
- Headquarters: Initially London; San Francisco office opened May 2024
- Name history: "Frontier AI Taskforce" → "AI Safety Institute" (2023) → "AI Security Institute" (February 2025)
- Role: Evaluates AI model capabilities; conducts safety testing
- Tooling: "open-sourced an AI safety tool called 'Inspect', which evaluates AI model capabilities such as reasoning and their degree of autonomy"
- Partnership: Agreed with US in April 2024 to collaborate on joint safety tests

## US counterpart
- Founded: November 2023
- Parent: National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)
- Renamed: "Center for AI Standards and Innovation" (CAISI, June 2025)
- Budget: $10M allocated (March 2024)
- Consortium: created February 2024 with "more than 200 organizations such as Google, Anthropic or Microsoft"

## Japan counterpart
- Founded: February 2024
- Parent: Information Technology Promotion Agency
- Staff: ~23 employees
