# Wikipedia: Microsoft Copilot
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_Copilot
Fetched: 2026-05-06

## Launch (verbatim)
"The service was introduced in February 2023 under the name Bing Chat" — initial release 2023-03-07.

## Underlying models (verbatim)
"Based on OpenAI's GPT-4 and GPT-5 series of large language models."
Architecture: "Microsoft Prometheus model" combining "Bing search index and results with OpenAI's GPT-4, GPT-4 Turbo, and GPT-4o foundational large language models."

## Rebrand (verbatim)
"On September 21, 2023, Microsoft began rebranding all variants of its Copilot to Microsoft Copilot."
"In November 2023, Bing Chat itself was being rebranded as Microsoft Copilot."

## Microsoft 365 Copilot
- Announced: 2023-03-16
- Pricing (as of July 2023): "US$30 per user, per month" for enterprise
- General availability: 2023-11-01

## Adoption (verbatim)
"One million people joined its waitlist within a span of 48 hours."
By May 2023: "Microsoft had broadened its reach to 600 customers who were willing to pay for early access."
