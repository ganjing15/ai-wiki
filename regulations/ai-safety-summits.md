# AI Safety Summits

## Source

## Bletchley (2023)
- **Dates**: 2023-11-01 to 2023-11-02
- **Location**: Bletchley Park, Milton Keynes, England

### Bletchley Declaration — primary text
Source: [gov.uk Bletchley Declaration](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/ai-safety-summit-2023-the-bletchley-declaration/the-bletchley-declaration-by-countries-attending-the-ai-safety-summit-1-2-november-2023). Raw: [bletchley-declaration.md](../raw/articles/bletchley-declaration.md).

Title (verbatim): "The Bletchley Declaration by Countries Attending the AI Safety Summit, 1-2 November 2023"

**28 signatories (verbatim list)**: Australia, Brazil, Canada, Chile, China, European Union, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Ireland, Israel, Italy, Japan, Kenya, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Netherlands, Nigeria, The Philippines, Republic of Korea, Rwanda, Singapore, Spain, Switzerland, Türkiye, Ukraine, United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, United States of America. (New Zealand joined 2024-10-23.)

Key principle (verbatim): "AI should be designed, developed, deployed, and used...in a manner that is safe, in such a way as to be human-centric, trustworthy and responsible"

Frontier-AI focus (verbatim): "potential for serious, even catastrophic, harm"

Commitment (verbatim): "an internationally inclusive network of scientific research on frontier AI safety"

## Seoul (2024) — primary text
Source: [gov.uk Seoul Summit](https://www.gov.uk/government/topical-events/ai-seoul-summit-2024) and the [Frontier AI Safety Commitments document](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/frontier-ai-safety-commitments-ai-seoul-summit-2024/frontier-ai-safety-commitments-ai-seoul-summit-2024). Raw: [seoul-frontier-commitments.md](../raw/articles/seoul-frontier-commitments.md).

- **Dates**: 2024-05-21 to 2024-05-22
- **Frontier AI Safety Commitments — 16 signatory organizations (verbatim)**: Amazon, Anthropic, Cohere, Google, G42, IBM, Inflection AI, Meta, Microsoft, Mistral AI, Naver, OpenAI, Samsung Electronics, Technology Innovation Institute, xAI, Zhipu.ai

Outcome 1 (verbatim): organizations must "assess the risks posed by their frontier models or systems across the AI lifecycle" and "set out thresholds at which severe risks posed by a model or system, unless adequately mitigated, would be deemed intolerable." They commit "not to deploy" models "if mitigations cannot be applied to keep risks below the thresholds."

## Paris (2025) — "AI Action Summit"
Source: [Wikipedia: AI Action Summit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AI_Action_Summit). Raw: [wikipedia-ai-action-summit.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-ai-action-summit.md).

- **Dates**: 2025-02-10 to 2025-02-11
- **Location**: Grand Palais, Paris

Declaration (verbatim): **58 countries** signed the "Statement on Inclusive and Sustainable Artificial Intelligence for People and the Planet."

> "The US and UK refused to sign the declaration on inclusive and sustainable AI."

The UK rationale (verbatim): the agreement "did not go far enough in defining global governance of AI and addressing concerns about its impact on national security."
