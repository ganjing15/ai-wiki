# AGI Risk

## Source
[Wikipedia: Existential risk from artificial intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Existential_risk_from_artificial_intelligence) → raw excerpt at [wikipedia-agi-risk.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-agi-risk.md).

## Definition (verbatim)
> "Existential risk from artificial intelligence, or AI x-risk, refers to the idea that substantial progress in artificial general intelligence (AGI) could lead to human extinction or an irreversible global catastrophe."

## Bostrom's superintelligence definition (verbatim)
> "Any intellect that greatly exceeds the cognitive performance of humans in virtually all domains of interest."

## Instrumental convergence — Russell (verbatim)
> "If you give it any goal whatsoever, it has a reason to preserve its own existence to achieve that goal."

## Alignment failure (verbatim)
> "Almost any technology has the potential to cause harm in the wrong hands, but with superintelligence, we have the new problem that the wrong hands might belong to the technology itself."

## Skeptics (verbatim from Wikipedia)
- **[[Yann LeCun]]**: "Those drives are programmed into our brain but there is absolutely no reason to build robots that have the same kind of drives."
- **Andrew Ng**: AI existential risk is "like worrying about overpopulation on Mars when we have not even set foot on the planet yet."
- **Timnit Gebru et al.**: discussion of existential risk "distracts from the immediate, ongoing harms from AI taking place today, such as data theft, worker exploitation, bias, and concentration of power."

## Major events (verbatim)
- **May 2023 CAIS Statement**: "Mitigating the risk of extinction from AI should be a global priority alongside other societal-scale risks such as pandemics and nuclear war."
- **March 2023 FLI Pause Letter**: called for a "six-month pause of any systems more powerful than GPT-4."

### FLI Pause Letter — primary source detail
Source: [Wikipedia: Pause Giant AI Experiments](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pause_Giant_AI_Experiments:_An_Open_Letter). Raw: [wikipedia-fli-pause-letter.md](../raw/articles/wikipedia-fli-pause-letter.md).

- "Published by the Future of Life Institute in March 2023"
- 30,000+ signatures including **Yoshua Bengio, Stuart Russell, Elon Musk, Steve Wozniak, Yuval Noah Harari**
- Sam Altman: letter was "missing most technical nuance about where we need the pause"
- Bill Gates declined to sign
- Outcome: "As of July 2024, a pause has not been realized"

## P(doom) figures (verbatim)
> "A 2022 expert survey found the majority believed there is a 10 percent or greater chance that human inability to control AI will cause an existential catastrophe."

> Median expectation in the same survey: "5–10% for the possibility of human extinction from artificial intelligence."
